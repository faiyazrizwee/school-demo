Our History

Founded in 1995, Green Valley International School has been a beacon of educational excellence for nearly three decades.

What started as a small initiative to provide quality education in the community has now grown into a premier institution with state-of-the-art facilities and a global outlook. Over the years, we have nurtured thousands of students who have gone on to excel in various fields across the world.

Our Vision

To create a community of lifelong learners who are compassionate, responsible, and innovative global citizens.

Our Mission

To provide a stimulating learning environment that fosters intellectual, social, and emotional growth through a rigorous and holistic curriculum.
