Flagship Program

Robotics & AI

From building simple bots to programming complex autonomous systems.

Our Robotics lab is equipped with the latest kits including LEGO Mindstorms, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi. Students learn to code and construct robots, participating in national and international competitions.

Programming & App Dev

Logic, algorithms, and the language of the future.

Students start with block-based coding and progress to Python, Java, and Web Development. The curriculum encourages creating real-world applications and solving computational problems.

Graphics & Animation

Bringing imagination to life through digital art.

Using industry-standard software, students explore graphic design, 3D modelling, and animation. From poster design to short films, creativity knows no bounds.

Sound Production

The art and science of audio engineering.

Our studio allows students to record, mix, and produce their own music and podcasts, understanding the technical aspects of sound.
