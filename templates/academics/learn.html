Early Years Foundation

Play-based learning where curiosity drives discovery.

Our pre-primary curriculum focuses on the holistic development of the child, integrating social, emotional, cognitive, and physical growth through interactive playful activities.

Primary Years

Building literacy, numeracy, and strong enquiry skills.

Students engage in project-based learning that encourages them to ask questions and find answers. The core subjects are supplemented by arts, sports, and language programs.

Middle School

Fostering independence and critical thinking.

The curriculum broadens to include specialized subjects, preparing students for higher academic challenges while nurturing their individual interests and talents.

Secondary School

Academic rigor and personal growth.

Students prepare for board examinations with focused guidance, while developing leadership skills and participating in a wide range of co-curricular activities.

Senior Secondary

Pathways to university and beyond.

We offer specialized streams in Science, Commerce, and Humanities, providing personalized career counseling and mentorship to help students achieve their goals.
