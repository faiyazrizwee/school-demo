How to Apply

We welcome students from all backgrounds who share our passion for learning.

1 Registration

Fill out the online enquiry form or visit the school admission office to collect the registration form. A registration fee of $50 is applicable.

2 Assessment / Interaction

For primary and above, a short assessment will be conducted to understand the student's grade readiness. For pre-primary, an interaction with parents and the child is scheduled.

3 Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive an admission offer letter. Parents must complete the formalities and pay the fees within the stipulated time to secure the seat.
